Our Educational Approach

At Kings Gate International School, our educational approach is designed to provide students with a well-rounded and enriching learning experience. We combine the esteemed British curriculum with a deep appreciation for Sri Lankan values to create a holistic educational journey.

Joining the Global Legacy of Cambridge Education

Cambridge International School holds a prestigious position as the first international school in Anuradhapura and the 16th in the country. Established as a respected educational institution in the region, it has garnered admiration from both the local community and across Sri Lanka.
While the original school temporarily closed its doors, it later reemerged as a Cambridge International School franchise. This transformation was not only about continuing the legacy of academic excellence but also about contributing to the broader educational landscape in the country.
As a franchise, Cambridge International School is dedicated to aiding other international schools in establishing their educational frameworks and foundations in Sri Lanka. By providing students with a British curriculum education, we empower the future of international education in the nation.
