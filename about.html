Welcome to Kings Gate International School

Kings Gate International School, located in the heart of Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka, is dedicated to delivering exceptional British curriculum education. We draw inspiration from the grandeur of Anuradhapura's kings, instilling values of majesty, leadership, discipline, character development, and a strong value system in our students.

Empowering Young Learners and Primary Students

At Kings Gate International School, our commitment lies in empowering young minds during their most formative years. As an institution catering specifically to preschool and primary school students, we offer a supportive and enriching educational environment for ages 2+ to 10+.

We deeply understand the pivotal role of early education in laying a robust foundation for lifelong learning. Our curriculum and teaching methods are meticulously tailored to address the unique needs and developmental stages of young children. This approach ensures that they embark on their educational journey with a strong sense of confidence and an innate curiosity.

Our dedicated team of educators is profoundly passionate about cultivating a love for learning and fostering an environment that encourages exploration and discovery within the classroom. We firmly believe that these early years are the fundamental building blocks for future academic success and the cultivation of responsible citizenship.

Explore how Kings Gate International School can provide your child with a nurturing, engaging, and supportive educational experience during their preschool and primary school years. Our aim is to set the stage for a bright academic future by igniting the spark of learning and excellence within each student.
