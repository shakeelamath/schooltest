Welcome to Kings Gate International School

Kings Gate International School is a new institution that has proudly joined the prestigious Cambridge International School Franchise. Located in the heart of Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka, we are committed to providing an exceptional British curriculum education while honouring the rich traditions of our island nation.

About Us

Our Affiliation with Cambridge International School Franchise.

As a part of the Cambridge International School Franchise, we draw from a legacy of academic excellence and innovation. The franchise, with a respected history as the first international school in Anuradhapura and the 16th in Sri Lanka, has garnered admiration from the local community and the country.

Our affiliation with the franchise allows us to provide students with a world-class British curriculum education recognized and respected worldwide.

Empowering Young Learners and Primary Students

At Kings Gate International School, our educational focus is centred on nurturing young minds during their most formative years. We cater specifically to preschool and primary school students, offering a supportive and enriching environment for ages 2+ to 10+

We understand the critical importance of early education in building a strong foundation for lifelong learning. Our curriculum and teaching methods are tailored to meet the unique needs and developmental stages of young children, ensuring that they embark on their educational journey with confidence and curiosity.

Our dedicated team of educators is passionate about fostering a love for learning and encouraging exploration and discovery in the classroom. We believe that these early years are the building blocks of future academic success and responsible citizenship.

Discover how Kings Gate International School can provide your child with a supportive and engaging educational experience during their preschool and primary school years.

Our Unique Educational Approach

At Kings Gate International School, we offer a blend of the esteemed British curriculum and the rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka.

This unique fusion creates a holistic educational experience that prepares our students for success in a diverse and interconnected world.

Our curriculum goes beyond academics, emphasising critical thinking, character development, and a global perspective.

We nurture well-rounded individuals who not only excel academically but also embrace the values and traditions of our island nation.
