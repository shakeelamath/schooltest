Discover Our Academic Excellence

At Kings Gate International School, we are dedicated to providing a world-class academic experience that prepares our students for success in a globalised world.

Pre School: Nurturing Young Minds (Ages 2+ to 4+)

Our Pre School program is designed to provide a warm and engaging environment for our youngest learners. It includes:

  • Nursery (2+): Our Nursery program welcomes little ones from the age of 24 months, providing a safe and supportive environment for their early educational experiences. Here, they learn through play, exploration, and social interaction, setting the stage for a lifelong love of learning.
  • Junior KG (3+): At Junior KG, we build on the foundations laid in the Nursery, encouraging children to explore and discover. Our curriculum is tailored to foster their cognitive and social development, ensuring they develop essential skills while having fun.
  • Senior KG (4+): Senior KG is the final step in our Pre School journey. Here, children continue to grow academically and socially, preparing them for a seamless transition into primary education.

Primary School: Building a Strong Foundation (Grade 1 to Grade 5)

Our Primary School program focuses on academic excellence and the development of character, leadership, and strong values. It includes:

  • Grade 1: The first step in building a strong academic foundation while instilling values and discipline.
  • Grade 2: Continued nurturing of young minds, fostering excellence in every student.
  • Grade 3: Emphasis on cultivating wisdom and innovation, instilling critical thinking skills and preparing students for an interconnected world.
  • Grade 4: Encouragement of leadership and character development, creating a supportive atmosphere for students to develop a strong value system and pride in their heritage.
  • Grade 5: Final preparation for excellence, equipping students with the skills and values to excel as they move on to the next phase of their educational journey.
