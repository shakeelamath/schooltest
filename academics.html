Our Curriculum

Our Affiliation with Cambridge International School Franchise.

We offer a comprehensive British curriculum that is recognized and respected worldwide. Our curriculum is rooted in tradition, ensuring that students receive a solid foundation in core subjects. However, we go beyond the basics to provide a well-rounded education that includes cultural studies, languages, and the arts.

Our educators are dedicated to creating dynamic and engaging learning experiences that cater to the unique needs of each student. We believe that education should be a journey of exploration and discovery, and our curriculum reflects this philosophy.

Academic Excellence

At Kings Gate International School, we uphold the rich tradition of Cambridge education, delivering academic excellence through British curriculum standards. Our students consistently achieve outstanding results, showcasing their knowledge and skills on a global stage.

Our commitment to academic excellence extends beyond standardised tests. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel academically while also embracing the cultural heritage and values of Anuradhapura.
